Abstract-This paper designs a scheme of traceability system for industrial equipment based on blockchain and Internet of Things identification. The internal traceability solution of the blockchain mainly records the key traceability information of industrial equipment and the digital summary of the detailed traceability information.

The traceability solution outside the blockchain mainly records detailed industrial equipment traceability information and digital summaries generated by the traceability information. The traceability system proposed in this paper ensures the authenticity of all traceable information and avoids the problem of blockchain data explosion.
